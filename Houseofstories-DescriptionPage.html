About the author and his house of stories


About the Author


Manan Kalra, a young and ambitious author, is 11 years old and is in grade 6. He was born in India. Some of his passions include reading books, playing chess, and doing coding. Fun Fact: He won an International Coding Competition at age 10. He is a smart, curious, and affectionate person, and loves writing mystery and adventure stories. He wrote his first book when he was 7, and has enjoyed writing books ever since. His favourite subjects are math and science.

About his house of stories


His house of stories was born when he had to create a story for a coding project. He decided he would combine many of the short sotries he had written into one, and thus Manan's House of Stories was born.'
